Big Changes in the Crow Family.
This family of crows is experiencing the devastating effects of the breakup of their family. One of the young crows narrates this story of love, accepting and celebrating differences, and the dreaded mention of divorce. The story is told in vivid color, the family members are recognizable, and you can easily interject your own story.
