In the tradition of Aesop, Fresh Fables bring child-like whimsy and adult wisdom to the struggles of the human condition. These beautifully illustrated stories teach an important lesson, foster discussion, or just give children a better understanding of their world. Fresh Fables present grown-up issues in a relatable way to help anyone adapt and thrive.
Chek out "The Dragon Who Lives At Our House" and "Big Changes In The Crow Faminly" two vibrant and educating illustrated stories.
